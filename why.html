My Goals

  1. Why do I want to learn this skill?

    It will prove to myself that I can learn new skills in my old age.

  2. What will I achieve if I learn this skill?

    I will open a door to a new future that I thought was beyond me. Hopefully, it will enable me to work better hours in better places, maybe even remotely from home.

  3. How will this skill change my life and my career?

    I will be happier in my life working for an IT firm instead of sweating or freezing to death in a factory making JCB's getting no satisfaction whatsoever.

  4. How will learning this skill impact the lives of my family, friends and coworkers?

    I am sure that my family and friends would be happy for me to make progress in my life, but maybe my coworkers will miss me a little.

    Gaining respect from anyone is an impressive feat and would be an added bonus if I did manage to acquire any.

  5. How will I feel if I never accomplish this?

    Gutted, a failure, deluded, devoid of hope and suicidal every time I get up before light to go to work in a factory.

  6. What would my life look like if I manage to accomplish this?

    Everyday would put a smile on my face knowing I didn't have to work in a place that I hate. I would be doing something that was challenging and giving me satisfaction. Doing something I enjoy, not just going through the same routines. I would definitely be a happier and more friendly person.

    Any monetary gains are second, but would be a very welcome bonus if acquired.
